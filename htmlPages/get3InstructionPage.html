Objective:
In this game, you will be asked to choose the word that seems the most out-of-place; the other words will be similar.

How to play:
In order to select your answer, drag the chosen word to the dashed box, or press a number key (1, 2, or 3).
Use your intuition.
The timer at the top of the page will count down from 60 seconds. Try and get as many as you can!
Don't be too hasty though: there is a streak bonus! The more you get right consecutively, the more points you get!

Scoring:
Each correct answer will get you 3 points, plus a bonus based on your streak.
The bonus is equal to your streak times 3.
For example, if you get three correct in a row the first answer will get you 3 points, the next will get you 6, and the final one will get you 9.
So, getting three correct consecutively will give you 18 points in total.
